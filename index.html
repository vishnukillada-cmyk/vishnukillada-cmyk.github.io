Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Featured on Season 3 Episode 16 of Bob's Cooking Show

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

Make the noodles: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine and cook until al dente, about 10 minutes. Reserve 1/2 cup of the cooking water, then drain well. Set aside.

Make the chicken: Season chicken breasts with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Heat olive oil over medium-high heat in a skillet. Add the chicken and cook 5‚Äì7 minutes per side until golden and cooked through (165¬∞F). Add 1 tablespoon butter while cooking. Rest chicken 3 minutes, then slice.

Veggie Stir Fry

Featured on Season 2 Episode 5 of Bob's Cooking Show

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions

Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add garlic and stir 30 seconds. Add vegetables and cook 5‚Äì7 minutes until tender-crisp. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce, stir to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil before serving. Serve with rice or noodles.

Tomato Basil Pasta

Featured on Season 1 Episode 12 of Bob's Cooking Show

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

Cook spaghetti according to package directions. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook garlic until fragrant. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Simmer 10‚Äì15 minutes. Stir in basil. Toss sauce with pasta and top with Parmesan.

Overnight Oats

Featured on Season 4 Episode 3 of Bob's Cooking Show

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk (any kind)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup fruit (berries, banana, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds (optional)

Instructions

In a jar or bowl, combine oats, milk, honey, and chia seeds. Stir well. Cover and refrigerate overnight. In the morning, top with fruit and enjoy cold or warm.
